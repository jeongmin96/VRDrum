virtualMIDI - one of virtual MIDI miniport driver implementation
 Virtual MIDI driver which can be executed on Windows 10(both 32- or 64bit)
The driver is currently part of the rtpMIDI-driver network-MIDI driver
 and the loopMIDI virtual loopback MIDI cable.
 But it can be used for other tasks as well.
